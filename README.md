#fooduck self service campus canteen 
fooduck is a website design to help service in campus canteen much easier. It represent as a self service web where instead of ordering food on the place you can order the food from the phone even when you are not there, 
it helps a student that has a short gap classes where they can order the food after finishing class and by the time they get there the food is ready or the user can choose the food to go from the app without needed to go in line.
